# 1.2 — Data Broker Source Map

> **TraceBurn Phase 1 Research**
> Created: 2026-04-01
> WBS Tasks: 1.2.1, 1.2.2, 1.2.3, 1.2.4
> **Note:** Opt-out URLs and timelines change frequently. Verify all links before automation. Last verified: 2026-04-01.

---

## Summary (Key Findings)

- **20 brokers documented** across consumer people-search, marketing data, and credit data arms.
- Opt-out automation is possible for ~11 brokers (fully or partially via web form); the remaining 9 require manual steps, email, phone, or legal requests.
- Re-listing risk is **high** for consumer people-search brokers (Spokeo, Whitepages, Radaris, FamilyTreeNow) — data frequently reappears within 30–90 days from upstream sources.
- Enterprise data brokers (Acxiom, Epsilon, Oracle, LexisNexis, Experian, Equifax non-credit) have the longest removal timelines (30–90 days) and often require written requests or official legal rights claims.
- **BeenVerified, TruthFinder, InstantCheckmate, PeopleLooker, USSearch** are all operated by the same parent company (PeopleConnect / Instant Checkmate LLC) — a single opt-out may cover all. Verify current policy.
- **Priority removal targets:** Spokeo, Whitepages, BeenVerified, MyLife, Radaris — these are highest-traffic and most likely to surface in Google results.
