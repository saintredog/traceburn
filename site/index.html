Why It Matters

Your data is out there.
More than you think.

It's for sale

Data brokers collect your name, home address, phone number, relatives, and purchase history — then sell it to anyone willing to pay. You never consented.

Removal is a maze

Every broker has a different opt-out process. Some need email confirmation. Some need phone calls. Doing this manually for 100+ sites takes days.

It keeps coming back

Brokers re-scrape public records regularly. Even after removal, your data can reappear within weeks. Most privacy services never check.

The Process

Three steps. All automated.

01

Find

TraceBurn searches 100+ data broker sites for your name, address, email, and phone. Every match is scored and ranked.

02

Remove

Automated opt-out submissions via three methods: browser automation, email requests, and guided manual steps for the stubborn ones.

03

Verify

TraceBurn re-checks every removed listing at 7, 30, and 90 days. If your data reappears, it removes it again — automatically.

Transparency

Every opt-out has a receipt.

When TraceBurn removes your data, it creates a timestamped proof record: a screenshot of the confirmation page, the HTTP response code, and a SHA-256 checksum. You can verify every single action.

  • Encrypted screenshot of confirmation page
  • UTC timestamp + broker name
  • SHA-256 checksum for tamper evidence
  • Exportable as PDF

Quick Start

Install TraceBurn

Runs entirely on your machine. No account, no cloud, no subscription.

Step 1 — Clone or Download
git clone https://github.com/saintredog/traceburn
Step 2 — Install
cd traceburn && ./install.sh
Step 3 — Set Up & Scan
traceburn init
traceburn scan
Requires: Python 3.9+ · macOS or Linux · ~200MB disk (Playwright)
